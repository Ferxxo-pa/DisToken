# Why I Built DisToken — And Why Digital Art Display is Broken

## The Problem Nobody Talks About

You spent $2,000 on an NFT. Maybe more. Where is it right now?

Sitting in a wallet. On a blockchain. Invisible.

The entire NFT market — $24.7 billion in 2022 trading volume — created a world where people own art they never look at. We turned art into financial instruments and forgot the whole point: **art is meant to be seen.**

Hardware digital frames tried to fix this. Tokenframe raised millions. Infinite Objects shipped physical displays. Meural (by Netgear) went premium.

Then Tokenframe went dark. Bricked hardware. Servers shut down. Thousands of frames turned into expensive paperweights.

**A hardware company can die. A URL can't be bricked.**

## What DisToken Actually Is

DisToken turns any screen into a digital art gallery. Your TV. Your tablet. A monitor at a coffee shop. A phone mounted on your wall. No app. No hardware. No account. Just a URL.

```
https://distoken.art/YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS
```

That's it. Enter any Ethereum, Solana, Base, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Tezos, or Bitcoin Ordinals wallet address and you get an instant, curated gallery of everything you own.

**8 chains. Zero friction. One link.**

## Why This Matters Beyond NFTs

The NFT market isn't dead — it's evolving. Art NFTs are becoming what they always should have been: **digital art you actually display.**

But here's the bigger play: as real-world assets (RWAs) get tokenized, as AI generates billions of images, as digital identity becomes visual — the need for "display any token on any screen" only grows.

DisToken isn't just an NFT viewer. It's the display layer for the tokenized internet.

## What's Built (Everything)

This isn't a concept. It's shipped:

- **Multi-chain support** — 8 blockchains, all major NFT standards
- **Phone remote** — Control your TV gallery from your phone via room code. No app needed.
- **Playlists & curation** — Pin favorites, hide pieces, create custom playlists, filter by collection
- **Kiosk mode** — Zero-chrome display for TVs, galleries, retail. Just add `?mode=kiosk`
- **Embed widget** — Drop a gallery into any website with one iframe tag
- **Offline cache** — Service worker stores 200+ images. Works without internet after first load.
- **PWA** — Install as a native app on any device
- **Visitor analytics** — Track views, sessions, dwell time on your gallery

## The Vision

**Phase 1 (Now):** Free display tool. Grow user base. Get NFT collectors actually looking at their art.

**Phase 2:** Featured Collections — browse curated collections (Art Blocks, CryptoPunks, Memes by 6529) without connecting a wallet. Discovery layer.

**Phase 3:** Connect wallet + buy. See art on your TV → buy it in one click from the display view. Reservoir SDK integration. We become a marketplace where the shopping experience IS the product experience.

**Phase 4:** Auction platform. Native bidding from the display view. English and Dutch auctions. The gallery becomes the auction house.
